THE MISSION

YOU ARE IN CONTROL OF YOUR LIFE

As long as you remain in a dream, you waste all the potential that God placed in you. No one can save you — you must save yourself. Many people think they will always get what they want from others. That is not true. There are times when you're down and need help, but others may not show up to give you the strength you need.

No one cares about you, so don't spend your time thinking that someone will save you. — Davin KITA

FINDING STRENGTH IN STRUGGLE

Sometimes we feel lost or uncomfortable, but these are the things that prepare us for greatness. When you feel lost, remember you have God in heaven who cares about all your problems. He allows them to bring you into a place of abundance.

I don't complain, even when it's tough. I celebrate because I am getting stronger. Even when people turn their backs on you, lie about you, or do wrong, I don't hate them. Because they give you an opportunity to reinvent yourself.

RESILIENCE & FORGIVENESS

They tried to bury me, but I rose again. I am here not for revenge, but for fulfillment. After all of that, I thank God for giving me strength through this experience. I know it is not easy to forget, but I have already forgiven them. I pray for them because I want everybody to win.

In this dark room, I realized I have been surrounded by negativity. I keep struggling until I prove to myself and to God that I can overcome. Who can be against me?

THE TRANSFORMATION

BUILDING IN THE DARKNESS

We are all made differently and each of us is good at something. In the different moments that life brings, look for something positive. No matter what you are going through, fight — don't let hell defeat you. Fight through it and speak about it when it is finished. That is how you become a real diamond.
